Active Minds

Active Minds is a mental health awareness organization that promotes education on mental health. Targetting young adults, the hopes of Active Minds is that they will spread awareness and change the conversations the younger generations have about mental health. It's very common to push feelings aside and not want to ask for help, but our generation can change that! There are places to donate on this website, so give it a look!
